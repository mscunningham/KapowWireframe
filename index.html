COMIC ENTHUSIASTS MAKING AND DISCUSSING COMICS TOGETHER

So what's this all about?

Throughout the year we enjoy the production of short, awesome comics and comic reviews.
Not everyone in Kapow is an artist. Many of us are also writers, editors, and just people who enjoy comics. Everybody contributes in some way or another, whether they aid in brainstorming, coloring flats, or providing moral support (very important). If any of this sounds interesting for you, consider joining us in Lien 1 on Saturdays from 3-5pm for snacks and comic follies.

KaPOW also goes out into the world to spread the love of comics with caricature nights in Bear's Den, guest speaking artist, and sometimes *wink wink* sometimes even conventions. So keep an eye out and come join in the comic club madness. We don't bite.

Sound like a good time?
